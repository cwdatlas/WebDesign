This site will give you an idea of what hides in the great abyss; from the the first to the fith layer

Another name for the Nakikabane is the Corps Reaper, which lives in the first and second layers. The Nakikabane can simulate the screams of their prey in use while hunting to lure their prey closer. As they do not have beaks they must use their tongues to rip meat out of their prey.

These Little creatures live in the 3rd layer of the Abyss. They are a great source of food while being easy to catch.

Hamashirama live in the oceans of the 5th layer: the sea of corpses. These fish can be made into hamashirama sushimi. This has been stated to be a great and tasty source of food in the 5th layer
