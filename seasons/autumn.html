Welcome to Mars

What can you do on Mars?

Its always harvest time on Mars! The high in iron dirt make it the best place for the Roanoke Genetics team to develope iron processing plants. You can walk through the great iron orchards and farms, you can even help pick iron fruit from the trees. The trees are all the colors of autumn you could ever want with their leaves which are high in iron. The spectrum of color goes from yellow to a deep burnt red. No place in the system can compete with the looks of the Iron fields of Mars. Thats not all, you know that dusty smell in the air when leaves are falling off the trees? Well, you wont find a place with a stronger smell! Its almost sufficating! What could be better?

We dont have Atumn on earth anymore, so to see a form of it in real life was amazing! Ill have to tell all my friends about this place! I never knew the people of the past had to deal with such a strong smell of dust! Its really admirable. -Kathy Morcov (Autumn Historian)
