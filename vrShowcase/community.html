The VR Communty

The VR community is a massive and diverse group of people from digital artists to the average Beat Saber player. All of these people love the medium no matter how much time they actually spend in it. There are a couple important things to know about the community like what goups you might like more. With all the diversity there is a group for you!

VR Chat

VR chat is a crazy world of strange avatars and even stanger people. If you have seen the movie the Ready Player one VRC would be the closest thing to that world that we have today. The things a person can do in VRC are Golf, playing pool, and many many other things. One of the best part of VRC is the ability to custimize yourself in looks and in personality. Many people change their voice to be a different person than they are IRL. I would recomend to check it out if you are up to experience the subculture.

Moddeling

To make all of those avatars a person needs to be able to moddel avatars and objects. This is a large part of the VRC community and in extension the VR community in general. Moddeling and other topics similar like Unity developement is a massive topic, so I wont go into detail, but I can show you a wireframe of my old avatar. As you can see on the wireframe there are many polygons. The Moddeler needs to be able to shape the avatar with those polygons, sometimes one by one in the case of objects.

ThrillSeeker

Thrilseeker is a large and influential Youtuber and Twitch streamer. He focuses on VR technology and its effects on the community. He also focuses on his comunity in discord and VR chat. If anyone would like to get into VR its a great Idea to watch some of Thrill's videos to get a good idea of what your needing to do.
