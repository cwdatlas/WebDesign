About Us

Welcome to Roanoke VR where we guid you though the large and exotic world of VR!

Here at Roanoke VR we showcase the overall vr world. A person first needs to buy a headset then if desired join one of the great communities here in VR. The large quanitity of VR headsets and styles gives us a chance to show newcomers the best headset for them. When they get into the VR world you have the chance to experience many different games like BeatSaber, Pavlov, and VR chat. Roanoke VR is happy to help you through these choices and find your community. Happy exploring!
