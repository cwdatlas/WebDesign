      HostWinds

      HostWind owns and maintaines all of their own servers and other network related hardware they also have redundancy threwout their system to make sure that your server will always be running.

      • Costs per package:

            Basic - $6.74
            Advanced - $8.24
            Ultimate - $9.74

      • Hosting Services:

        HostWind offers business and personal webhosting, business costing more. Each package comes with Ulimited Disk Space and Unlimited bandwidth.

      • Domain Services:

            Basic - 1 domain
            Advanced - 4 domains
            Ultimate - Ulimited domains

        HostGator

        HostGator is a website hosting service, but more geared tward long term users, so they have plans that can easily scail with what the user would like. It's more for business use than HostWind, and is cheaper with mostly the same atrebutes. These are three diffrent services at thier lowest teir to give an idea of the diverse market HostGator provides to.

        • Costs per package:

              Shared Hosting - $2.75/month
              Website builder - $3.84/month
              Dedicated Hosting - $89.98/month

        • Hosting Services:

          HostGator has unlimited bandwidth for all services, and has far more comprehensive services compared to its competitors. Shared hosting is a cheap and effective way to have a website for any person. The Website builder is a good tool to create and host a website easily and without prior Web Design knowledge The Dedicated Hosting package provides a server istelf for a person to use as they see fit with a maximum storage of 1T.

        • Domain Services:

              Shared Hosting - 1 domain for the first year
              Website Builder - 1 domain (maybe only for first year)
              Dedicated Hosting - No domain included
